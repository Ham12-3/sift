Where is the JWT secret defined, and why did the review flag it?
AL

The secret is defined inline in src/auth.ts at line 11:

src/auth.ts
const secret = 's3cr3t-dev-key';
jwt.sign(payload, secret, { expiresIn: '2h' });

It's flagged Critical because the secret is committed to source control. Anyone with repo access can forge valid tokens. Move it to process.env.JWT_SECRET and rotate the exposed value.
